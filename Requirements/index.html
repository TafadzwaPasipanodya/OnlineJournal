Online Journal

App Description

An online note taking tool that also serves as a digital journal. It allows students to share notes with other students that are taking the same courses. It would also provide a calendar for student to post student sessions and events. The note taking option provides multiple layouts for note-taking depending the student's preference.

Target Audience

The target audience will mainly be students.
